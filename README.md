# SA Retail Sales Dashboard 

![Power BI](https://img.shields.io/badge/Power_BI-F2C811?style=flat&logo=powerbi&logoColor=black)
![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Complete-green)

An interactive Power BI dashboard analysing retail sales performance 
across product categories, regions, and time periods.

---

##  Problem Statement

Retail managers need a fast, visual way to track revenue, identify 
top-performing products, and spot monthly sales trends — without 
digging through raw spreadsheets.

---

##  Dataset

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Source | Kaggle — Superstore Sales Dataset |
| Size | 9,800 rows × 18 columns |
| Key fields | Sales, Profit, Category, Region, Order Date |

---

##  Dashboard Features

-  Total Revenue, Orders & Profit KPI cards
-  Sales breakdown by Product Category
-  Monthly Revenue Trend (line chart)
-  Sales distribution by Region
-  Top 10 Best-Selling Products

## Key Insights

- The Technology category generates the highest revenue
- Q4 consistently shows the strongest sales performance
- The West region leads in total sales volume
